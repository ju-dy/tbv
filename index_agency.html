About our company

We like nice weather. That's why we left the cold and hopped on a plane (okay, many planes) to the hottest place we could imagine...Singapore. In between sitting fon the beach and eating street food, we've been known to do some work, sometimes. We believe great products transcend national and cultural borders. If we like it, we hope you do too!

Our team has gathered from around the world -- and before coming together, we have imagined, built, and managed companies that have made a difference across many different industries. We are now building technology that sits at the intersection of the latests trends in mobile applications, and we have passionately believe it will create value for the community.
